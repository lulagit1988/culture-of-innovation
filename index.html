Culture of Innovation

Amazon's Culture of Innovation

Amazon has a peculiar approach to innovation that is intrinsically linked to how we use technology and organize our teams. In this session, we share with customers best practices and approaches to how we sustain innovation at scale through four interdependent elements: culture, mechanisms, architecture, and organization.

We dive deep on components including Leadership Principles; building sustainable flywheels; our narrative culture and democratizing innovation through Working Backwards; how AWS enables experimentation and rapid launch and learning; our two-pizza team organizational approach; and much more.
